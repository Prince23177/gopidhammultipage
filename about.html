About Gopi Dham

Welcome to Your Second Home

A Peaceful Retreat
in the Holy City

Gopi Dham is more than just a hotel; it is a spiritual ashram designed to give you peace of mind. Located in a quiet area near Raiwala, away from the extreme rush of the city, we provide a clean, secure, and family-friendly environment.

Start your mornings with devotion at our beautifully maintained In-House Shivling Temple. Whether you choose our Premium AC rooms for summer comfort or our well-ventilated Non-AC rooms, we ensure a comfortable stay within your budget.

The Location

Experience Haridwar

Haridwar, the gateway to the Gods, is a city where spirituality flows with the river Ganges. Staying at Gopi Dham allows you to experience this divine energy perfectly.

  • Har Ki Paudi (5 KM): Attend the mesmerizing evening Ganga Aarti easily.
  • Raiwala Station (2 KM): Excellent connectivity away from main city traffic.
  • Peaceful Environment: Enjoy the fresh air and serene atmosphere, ideal for meditation and yoga.
